The Australian Federal Government provides substantial rebates when you choose to install a solar PV system at your property. The rebate amount is based on the size of the system purchased and is deducted from the upfront cost of the solar system by your chosen solar provider. In 2024 you can expect a $2,520 or more reduction for a 6.6kW system, a common size for QLD homes.
